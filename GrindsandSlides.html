Grinds and Slides

Backside and Frontside: The Difference

Similar to Flatground Tricks, Grinds and Slides can be performed in all four stances. On top of that, every Slide or Grind can be done either "Backside" (BS) or "Frontside" (FS). This refers to the obstacles position relative to the skateboarder.

If a trick is performed, while the back of the skateboarder is facing the obstacle, it is called backside. If the chest is facing the obstacle, it is called frontside. Easy!
